What is water monitoring system?

A water monitoring system is a set of tools and processes used to measure and analyze water parameters. It includes sensors and instruments that collect data on factors like temperature, pH, and turbidity. The data is transmitted for analysis and can enable real-time monitoring and quick response to changes. Water monitoring systems generate reports and support decision-making for water resource management. Their purpose is to ensure the sustainable use of water resources and protect the environment and human health.
